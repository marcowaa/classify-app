# Classify — Review Response Templates

## Response Philosophy
- Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours (critical: within 4 hours)
- Always personalize — never copy-paste verbatim without tweaking
- Empathetic tone: we are a family company, speaking to families
- For negative reviews: acknowledge, apologize if warranted, offer a solution path
- Always end with an invitation to contact support

## 🔧 Escalation Protocol

### When to escalate to development team immediately:
- Multiple reviews mentioning same bug (3+ in 48h)
- Data loss reports
- Payment/billing issues
- Privacy/data concerns
- Inappropriate content reports

### Response time targets:
| Rating | Target Response Time |
|--------|---------------------|
| ⭐ | 2 hours |
| ⭐⭐ | 4 hours |
| ⭐⭐⭐ | 8 hours |

## 📊 Weekly Review Monitoring Checklist

```
□ Check Play Console for new reviews (daily)
□ Respond to all 1-2 star reviews (same day)
□ Log all reviews in tracking spreadsheet
□ Identify patterns/recurring issues
□ Share critical feedback with dev team
□ Update templates when new issue patterns emerge
□ Report weekly review sentiment to leadership
□ Celebrate positive reviews with the team
```
