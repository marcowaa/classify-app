# Classify — Google Play Launch Plan 2026

## Executive Summary

Full-scale Google Play launch targeting MENA families with children ages 5–12. Primary markets: Saudi Arabia, UAE, Egypt, Algeria, Morocco. Secondary: Kuwait, Qatar, Jordan, Tunisia, Turkey.

**Launch Target Date**: Q2 2026 (Ramadan post-Eid window — ideal for family app spending)
**Goal**: 10,000 downloads in first 30 days, 4.5+ star rating

---

## Pre-Launch Phase (T–4 Weeks)

### Week –4: Foundation
- [ ] Submit app to Google Play Console review
- [ ] Complete all policy documents (content rating, data safety, target audience)
- [ ] Upload all screenshots (phone, tablet 7", tablet 10")
- [ ] Upload feature graphic (main + seasonal Ramadan version)
- [ ] Submit app icon (A/B test 2 variants from day 1)
- [ ] Set up custom store listings for: Saudi AR, Gulf AR, North Africa AR, Morocco FR, Turkey TR
- [ ] Configure Google Play Families program enrollment
- [ ] Set up Firebase Analytics for conversion tracking
- [ ] Create Google Play Developer account verified payment methods

### Week –3: Content & Community
- [ ] Publish pre-launch landing page with email capture
- [ ] Set up social media accounts: Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok (Arabic content)
- [ ] Begin teaser campaign: "شيء جديد قادم لأطفالكم 🌳"
- [ ] Reach out to 20 Arabic parenting influencers (micro: 10K–100K followers)
- [ ] Submit to 5 Arabic app review blogs/YouTube channels
- [ ] Prepare press kit (screenshots, logo, description, founder bio)
- [ ] Translate all marketing materials to French (Maghreb campaign)

### Week –2: Beta & Feedback
- [ ] Open closed beta: invite 100 families from email list
- [ ] Collect beta feedback via Google Forms
- [ ] Fix critical bugs reported by beta testers
- [ ] Optimize app size (target: under 50 MB)
- [ ] Test on low-end devices (Android 6.0, 2GB RAM)
- [ ] Finalize promo video (upload to Google Play + YouTube)

### Week –1: Final Prep
- [ ] Submit final APK/AAB to Play Store
- [ ] Confirm all store listing assets are approved
- [ ] Brief customer support team on FAQ responses
- [ ] Schedule social media posts for launch day
- [ ] Prepare launch-day email to mailing list
- [ ] Set up review response templates in console
- [ ] Final QA: test purchase flow, parental controls, onboarding

## Launch Week (Week 1)

### Day 1 — Launch Day 🚀
**Morning (8:00 AM Gulf time)**
- [ ] Publish app to Google Play (all regions simultaneously)
- [ ] Send launch email to mailing list
- [ ] Post launch announcement on all social channels
- [ ] Notify all beta testers to leave reviews

**Content to post:**
- Instagram: Launch video (15s cut of promo video)
- Twitter/X: Thread explaining key features
- TikTok: "شجرة طفلك تحتاجك" challenge seed video
- WhatsApp/Telegram: Family group shareable card

### Day 2–3 — Momentum
- [ ] Respond to ALL early reviews (within 4 hours)
- [ ] Share user screenshots/testimonials on social
- [ ] Monitor crash reports in Play Console
- [ ] Send reminder to influencers who haven't posted yet
- [ ] Post FAQ content addressing common questions

### Day 4–5 — Push
- [ ] Launch Google UAC (Universal App Campaign) — Budget: $500/day
  - Target: Parents 25–45 in SA, UAE, EG, DZ, MA
  - Focus: Installs campaign
- [ ] Launch Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram) — Budget: $300/day
  - Audience: Parents with children 5–12
  - Creative: Tree growing video + before/after

### Day 6–7 — Review & Iterate
- [ ] Analyze: Conversion rate, store listing CTR, install sources
- [ ] Check ASO: Are keywords ranking?
- [ ] Respond to all reviews
- [ ] Adjust ad targeting based on Day 1–5 data
- [ ] Identify top-performing store listing creative

## Week 2–4 Goals

### Week 2: Optimization
- **Target**: 3,000 total installs
- Run A/B test on icon variants (Google Play Experiments)
- Run A/B test on screenshots (test 2 different orders)
- Monitor D1/D7 retention rates (target: D1 > 40%, D7 > 20%)
- Reach out to Arabic education media for coverage
- Submit app to Arabic app awards/lists

### Week 3: Growth
- **Target**: 6,000 total installs
- Launch referral program: "ادعُ صديق واربح جوهرات"
- Publish blog content on Arabic parenting sites
- Start YouTube channel with weekly "نصائح التعليم" content
- Analyze which keywords are driving organic installs

### Week 4: Retention
- **Target**: 10,000 total installs
- Launch in-app push notification re-engagement campaign
- Send Day 14 email with tips for inactive users
- Feature "قصة نجاح" success story on social media
- Review first month KPIs and adjust 3-month plan

## Influencer Partnership Plan

### Tier 1: Mega Influencers (1M+ followers) — 2 partnerships
- Budget: $3,000–$5,000 per post
- Goal: Awareness spike at launch
- Format: 60-second dedicated app review

### Tier 2: Mid-Tier (100K–1M followers) — 5 partnerships
- Budget: $500–$1,500 per post
- Goal: Credibility + targeted reach
- Format: Story series + reel

### Tier 3: Micro-Influencers (10K–100K followers) — 15 partnerships
- Budget: Product access (free premium) or $100–$300
- Goal: Authentic community engagement
- Format: Organic-style sharing post

### Target Influencer Categories
- Arabic mom bloggers ("ماما بلوغر")
- Arabic education/homeschool accounts
- Islamic parenting accounts
- Gulf family lifestyle channels
- Arabic children's content creators
