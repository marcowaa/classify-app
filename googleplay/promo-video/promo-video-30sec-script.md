# Classify — Google Play Promo Video Script (30 seconds)

## Overview
- **Duration**: 30 seconds
- **Format**: 9:16 vertical (1080×1920) for Google Play featured video
- **Language**: Arabic (primary) with English subtitle option
- **Target audience**: Parents of children ages 5–12

## Production Notes

### Visual Style
- **Color palette**: Deep purple (#1E1B4B) → bright green (#22C55E) → golden (#FDE68A)
- **Typography**: Cairo Bold/Black (Arabic), Inter Bold (English)
- **Animations**: Smooth 60fps, no jarring cuts
- **Screen recordings**: Real app UI (or high-fidelity mockups from HTML files)

### Audio Direction
- **Music**: Warm, optimistic, Middle Eastern-influenced background track
- **Voiceover**: Warm female Arabic voice, energetic but trustworthy tone
- **SFX**: Subtle — notification chimes, growth sounds, achievement fanfare

### Localization Versions
| Version | Language | Duration | Target Market |
|---------|----------|----------|---------------|
| v1-ar   | Arabic   | 30s      | All MENA      |
| v2-fr   | French   | 30s      | Maghreb       |
| v3-en   | English  | 30s      | International |
| v4-tr   | Turkish  | 30s      | Turkey        |

### Google Play Video Requirements
- Min resolution: 1080×1920 (portrait) or 1920×1080 (landscape)
- Format: MP4, H.264
- Max file size: 100 MB
- Duration: 30 seconds recommended (max 120s)
- Must not show device frame overlaid on another device frame
